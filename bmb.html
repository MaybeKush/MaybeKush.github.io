BMB - Box Multiplayer Battle

What is BMB, you ask?

BMB - Box Multiplayer Battle is a fast-paced action game. This game is made to spend some time with friends and family.

Family time? Play BMB. Have friends? Play BMB. Alone? Play BMB.

Play this game anytime, anywhere, with anyone.

Key features :

- Simple squares, easy on eyes.
- Variety of colors for a cheerful environment.
- Fast-paced.
- Multiplayer.
- Many weapons.
- Customizable room settings.

What role did I play in making of the game?

Well, I did everything, every single thing is made by me because I decided to make this game alone. Everything except sounds, some sounds are royalty free and downloaded from the internet because why not? Except that, everything, including multiplayer networking, fixing bugs, UI, icons, the pixel art of guns and effects all is done by me.
One thing is worth mentioning, that the AI, the base AI is not done by me either, I used the A Start AI package in unity to find the ways but, that doesn't mean everything AI was done by that package, I still needed to make the bot go to the path that AI found, fixed some bugs, and made how they should shoot so they are neither over powerful nor boring to play with. All that package did was to find a path among a grid, I could have done that myself but I decided to save some time and used an already built system.
