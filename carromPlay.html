Carrom Play

What is Carrom Play, you ask?

Carrom Play is a sport-based tabletop & carrom board disc games of Indian version of pool disk game . Don't miss out on this fun and relaxing carrom games!

Carrom Play is a real-time, family-friendly, online carrom board game that will revive your childhood memories. play the World best carrom board games & be a carrom star. Create a carrom club & be a king of online carrom disc game.

Carrom Play game has in total 3 modes. So, nothing can refrain you from playing this most loved ceram pool game ever even if there is no internet or WiFi connection.

Carrom Mode: Careme board is more like a classic board carram pool disk game of carroms, where you must pocket the queen and cover it with one of your pucks in caram dice pool.

Pool Disk: This mode allows you to play cramm board without queen, so you can straight away pocket all your pucks and win the carrom games.

2 vs 2 mode: A multiplayer mode where two teams of two players will play against each other as a carrom board disc game.

Play Offline: You can play this mode even if you don't have the internet or WiFi connection. you can play ceram bod game with our AI throughout 3 levels i.e. Easy, Medium & Hard.

If you have ever thought about playing carrom games a real table, Carrom Play is the perfect way to pick your favorite carrom poll game.

What role did I play in making of the game?

I was yet again incharge of multiplayer here using Photon Unity Networking 2, aka PUN 2. Though in this project I wasn't working from the beginning, they added me to fix bugs but I ended up remaking multiplayer because the last one was not good. I simplified somethings, removed unneccessary things, added an improved and optimized code for multiplayer which worked flawlessly... after debugging a thousand bugs of course. I am glad that I was able to do all the things in the limited time span, I don't remember it exactly but it had quite short time limit too.
